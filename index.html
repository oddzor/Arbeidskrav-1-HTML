Odd Grimholt - Frontend Developer (student)

I'm a 32-year-old man(child) from Sætre - famously known as the center of the universe.

I went to high school in Røyken studying media and communications. During this time I also started working at Specsavers
which would become a part of my life for years to come.

After high school, I decided to take on an entirely different adventure.I enlisted in the military at GSV, where I dedicated a year to protecting
Norway's northern frontier, at the Russian border.

After my military service, I set my sights on the skies and traveled all the way to Florida, USA, to pursue a career in aviation.
At Phoenix East Aviation, I not only earned my commercial pilot's license but also had the privilege of sharing my
knowledge and expertise as an instructor.

4 years later, returning to Norway, I continued my journey in aviation while converting my certification to european standards at
OSM Aviation Academy. It was a period of growth and learning, which eventually led me to a new chapter in my career.

After a few years at Specsavers to stay financially afloat I was hired at Pilot Flight Academy as a flight instructor,
then I was sent down to Airwin Academy in Hungary to reinstate my instructor licenses.
After this I continued my journey, very much enjoying it. It is at times stressful, dangerous and exhausting but also
very rewarding to be given such responsibility to not only teach, but to keep both myself, the student and
the (costly) hardware safe in the some times extreme weather in Norway.

In 2023, I was relieved of my position at Pilot Flight Academy and had (for some time) been considering a career change and getting
my feet wet in the world of technology. My interest in tech, combined with my desire to constantly push my boundaries,
led me to enroll as a student in the Frontend Developer course at Gokstad Akademiet.

I'm set to complete this program by the summer of 2025, and I couldn't be more excited about the future possibilities it holds.
Beyond my professional pursuits, I'm an adrenaline junkie at heart. I find immense joy in gaming, lifting weights, skateboarding,
and partaking in various adrenaline-pumping sports.

When I'm not pushing my limits, I enjoy relaxing with movies and series, staying updated on the latest tech trends,
and reconnecting with the beauty of nature. Life is an adventure, and I'm on a constant quest to explore new horizons,
both professionally and personally. Whether it's soaring through the skies or writing code for the future,
I'm always ready to embrace the next exciting challenge life has in store for me.

- Odd "OpenAI" Grimholt
